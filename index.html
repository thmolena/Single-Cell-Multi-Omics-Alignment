Single Cell Multi Omics Alignment

In this project, I designed novel supervised algorithms for performing cell-to-cell alignment of single-cell multi-omic datasets. The datasets were from https://rsinghlab.github.io/SCOT/. I also performed line by line comparison with the SCOT algorithm to show that my novel supervised algorithms perform better alignment.
